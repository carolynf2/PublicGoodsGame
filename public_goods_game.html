Economic Theory Concepts

Public Goods Game: This experiment demonstrates the tension between individual rationality and collective welfare. Each player faces a choice between keeping tokens (guaranteed return) or contributing to a shared pool that benefits everyone.

Free Rider Problem: While cooperation maximizes group welfare, individuals have incentive to "free ride" on others' contributions while keeping their own tokens.

Nash Equilibrium: In the basic game, the theoretical prediction is zero contribution from all players, yet real behavior often shows initial cooperation that declines over time.
